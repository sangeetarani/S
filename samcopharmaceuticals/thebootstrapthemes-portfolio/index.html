Samco Pharmaceuticals

Samco Pharmaceuticals is a partnership firm, company registered and has its Headquarter in Bangalore. Samco Pharmaceuticals, established in the year 2016.

We have a wide range of General molecule products to Diabetic products. We are currently present in Bangalore, Kolar, Chintamani, Ramnagaram, Malur, and other surrounding areas. We also have our presence in Gurgaon, Lucknow, Kanpur, Aligarh, Basti, Rewari, Pataudi.



Social Causes

Samco Pharmaceuticals have conducted medical camps (Diabetic and Hypertension) at multiple locations. across Bangalore, Ramnagaram, Hoskote, Kolar and Chintamani. We provide 5 -10 days free sampling to the people who require medication. For people who cannot afford medication at all, we give them free medication for life.
